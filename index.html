About

My journey into software development began with an introductory programming course as an undergraduate in Mechanical Engineering. The course covered strings, integers, loops and conditionals in VBA.

Years later I found myself with a rather complicated problem in Excel. I dusted off those VBA skills and realized that I didn’t know much. Google introduced me to Stack Overflow, and through both I learned about string manipulation, lists, dictionaries, and classes. This enabled me to solve the problem I was working on and provided solutions to two more.

After deciding to make a career change to software development, I enrolled in Eleven Fifty Academy’s 12-week immersive software development course in January 2020. This course has allowed me to build on my existing knowledge, learn new skills and develop better coding practices.
